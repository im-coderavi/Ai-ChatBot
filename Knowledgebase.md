## Day-to-Day Responsibilities

### Core Duties

1. **Vehicle Operation**
   - Safely operate company-provided delivery vehicle
   - Follow most efficient routes for timely deliveries
   - Adhere to all traffic laws and safety regulations
   - Maintain vehicle cleanliness

2. **Package Handling**
   - Load and unload packages from delivery vehicle
   - Ensure packages maintain their condition during transport
   - Lift and carry packages up to 150 lbs
   - Deliver to front door or designated access point

3. **Delivery Execution**
   - Plan and follow efficient delivery routes
   - Make timely and accurate deliveries (150 miles daily average)
   - Verify accuracy of packages
   - Complete proper documentation for each delivery

4. **Customer Interaction**
   - Provide exceptional customer service
   - Be polite, professional, and accommodating
   - Represent company positively at all times
   - Maintain professional image

5. **Communication & Coordination**
   - Collaborate with dispatch team
   - Optimize delivery schedules
   - Communicate delays or issues promptly
   - Report vehicle malfunctions, accidents, or violations immediately

6. **Compliance**
   - Adhere to company policies and procedures
   - Follow safety protocols
   - Maintain delivery operation standards

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Pay & Compensation

**Q: How is pay calculated?**  
A: Combination of hourly rate ($18-20/hour based on experience) plus per-stop bonuses. You also receive safety bonuses for maintaining a clean driving record.

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: Every Friday via direct deposit (weekly pay).

**Q: What determines starting pay - $18 or $20/hour?**  
A: Based on your prior experience in delivery, courier services, or professional driving. More experience = higher starting rate.

**Q: How much can I earn with bonuses?**  
A: Stop bonuses and safety bonuses can add several hundred dollars per month depending on performance.

**Q: Is overtime guaranteed?**  
A: Overtime is available 1-2 days per week but varies based on business needs, especially during peak seasons.

### Schedule & Work-Life Balance

**Q: What is the typical work schedule?**  
A: 4 days per week including 1 weekend day, with 1-2 days of overtime available. Shifts are typically 10 hours starting at 7:30 AM.

**Q: Which weekend day do I work?**  
A: Varies by route assignment - could be Saturday or Sunday, determined during hiring.

**Q: Can I request specific days off?**  
A: Discuss scheduling needs during the interview process. PTO is available after 90 days.

**Q: What if I can't work weekends?**  
A: Weekend availability is a requirement for this position. If weekends don't work, this role may not be a fit.

### Job Requirements

**Q: Do I need a CDL?**  
A: No! A standard valid driver's license is sufficient.

**Q: What if I have one speeding ticket?**  
A: A single minor traffic violation typically won't disqualify you. Multiple violations or serious infractions (DUI, reckless driving) are disqualifying.

**Q: How clean does my driving record need to be?**  
A: No major violations, accidents, or suspensions in the past 3 years. Clean record is ideal.

**Q: Can I lift 150 lbs by myself?**  
A: You need to be able to safely handle packages up to 150 lbs. This involves lifting, bending, and carrying to delivery points.

**Q: What about the background check?**  
A: Standard employment background check. Most criminal histories may not be disqualifying depending on nature, severity, and time elapsed. Serious violent crimes or recent felonies may disqualify.

### Benefits & Training

**Q: When do benefits start?**  
A: Health insurance and other benefits typically begin after 90 days of full-time employment. Specific details provided during hiring.

**Q: What does training involve?**  
A: 1-2 weeks paid training ($15/hour) covering vehicle operation, delivery procedures, safety protocols, route planning, and customer service.

**Q: What about PTO?**  
A: 5 days PTO after 90 days of full-time employment, can be used for vacation or sick time.

**Q: Do you hire seasonal workers?**  
A: Yes! Seasonal drivers who perform well have opportunity to transition to permanent positions.

### Military & Veterans

**Q: Do you offer preference to veterans?**  
A: Yes! Military and veteran applicants are strongly encouraged to apply. We value the skills, discipline, and work ethic veterans bring.

**Q: Do military skills transfer to this role?**  
A: Absolutely. Time management, following procedures, safety consciousness, and ability to work independently are all highly valued.
